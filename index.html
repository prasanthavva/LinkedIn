About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Certified Salesforce Developer, Administrator, and Service Cloud Consultant currently serving as a Senior Developer/Data Analyst at USAA in San Antonio, Texas. With a career spanning over a decade in enterprise software, I specialize in building robust, scalable Salesforce solutions in Lightning environments.

My expertise covers the full Salesforce development lifecycle — from custom Apex classes and SOQL queries, to Platform Events with Publish-Subscribe architecture, to intuitive Visualforce page design. I'm comfortable in both development and administration roles and enjoy bridging technical complexity with business needs.

Beyond Salesforce, I've been actively expanding into AI & Generative AI, holding five certificates in areas including Agentic AI, NLP, and Prompt Engineering.
